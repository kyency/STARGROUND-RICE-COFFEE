Our Story

STARGROUND emerged from a collective vision shared by six individuals eager to address a market need. Inspired by a prevailing trend, they passionately delved into the world of rice coffee, creating the brand with a dual significance.

Named after a pivotal group member, the term "star," and incorporating the past tense of "grind" into "ground," the brand uniquely captures both personal connection and the product's creation process. STARGROUND stands as a testament to purpose-driven entrepreneurship and innovative thinking.

Learn More

Our Vision

Starground Coffee envisions delivering quality and happiness to every Filipino through top-notch varieties of rice coffee with a traditional touch. Our commitment extends beyond coffee, emphasizing excellence in customer service, employee welfare, environmental protection, and community engagement, defining the essence of Starground Coffee.
